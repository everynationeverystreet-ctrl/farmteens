Our Story

Rooted in Purpose. Driven by Passion.

Farmteens Uganda (PTY) LTD was founded by young people with a vision to transform agriculture, empower communities, and create sustainable livelihoods.

We believe in the power of youth, the richness of our land, and the potential of working together.

Through training, innovation, and integrity, we are cultivating a future where people and nature thrive — together.
